Rebranding Dr Nut

Refreshing a beloved brand

Dr. Nut was a pop that came out on the market in the 1960s. Made of almonds, its particular flavor made it popular among children. As an idea to rebrand this popular drink, we created a beer for those kids that are nowadays grown-ups, keeping almond flavor to remind them of a piece of their childhood. With the motivation of catching a bigger piece of the market, more flavors came out to refresh and enjoy a new market. From logo reinvention to packaging design to a dynamic and vivid website, all were designed and developed in this project.

Keeping Dr. Nut essence, every label has a spot in New Orleans, bringing to mind the place where it was created.
