Street Photography

Why Street Photography?

This project was developed in my first year as a student of Interactive Media Design at Fanshawe College for a Digital Media course. All students were learning DSRL cameras usage as well as photo editing with Photoshop and Illustrator, our theme was open but I chose Street Photography because it's a way to learn more about a place, through its people, street, daily activities, businesses, among different variables. At that time, I was a new comer in Canada who was interested in learn about Canadian culture and developing this project gave me the opportunity to explore a new methodology as well as a new culture.

Tools

- Camera DSRL Nikon Z 6ii with Z 50mm f/1.8 S Lens - FULL FRAME Mirrorless Camera.

- Aperture and shutter modes were used to developed this project.

- Photoshop and Lightroom were used for photo editing.
