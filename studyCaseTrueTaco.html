True Taco Photoshoot

True Taco

The primary goal of the meticulously organized True Taco photo shoot was to refresh the restaurant's website and social media visuals. Every stage of the process, from market trend analysis brainstorming sessions to the meticulous dedication to detail during execution, was designed to present True Taco as a modern and attractive international dining destination in London Ontario. Each choice, from picking vivid backgrounds to arranging tasty food, was carefully considered to reflect the restaurant's lively atmosphere and delightful menu.
Careful editing techniques were used in post-production to enhance color and composition, making every shot display the happy spirit of True Taco. The intention was to draw in new clients by adding dynamic energy and vibrant aesthetics to the photos.

Tools

- Camera DSRL Nikon Z 6ii with Z 50mm f/1.8 S Lens - FULL FRAME Mirrorless Camera.

- Aperture and shutter modes were used to developed this project.

- Photoshop and Lightroom were used for photo editing.

Results

Raw images taken with Aperture and Shutter mode without photo editing.
